### What is black masterbatch used for?
Black masterbatch is used in plastic manufacturing to provide UV protection, black coloration and improved durability.

### Which industries use black masterbatch?
Water tank manufacturing, agriculture piping, packaging and automotive industries commonly use black masterbatch.

### Why is UV resistance important in black masterbatch?
UV resistance helps outdoor plastic products maintain long-term durability and weather protection.
